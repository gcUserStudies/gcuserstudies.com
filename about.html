About

gcUserStudies is a hobby project: a set of custom Sierra Chart studies I build for my own trading and share for anyone who finds them useful. It is maintained on a best-effort basis, with occasional updates as time allows. There are no paid tiers, nothing on this site collects data, and there is no service to operate. The current studies are closed-source, while the older studies on the legacy page are open-source with full code on GitHub. If something here is useful to you, great; please test in simulation first and use everything at your own risk.

If you would like to support the project, you can contribute here.

Trading futures and other instruments involves substantial risk of loss and is not suitable for everyone. Nothing on this site is financial advice.
